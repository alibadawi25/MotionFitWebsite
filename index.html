The tech

Real motion capture,
no wearables

One ordinary webcam is all it takes. MotionFit reads your whole body in real time — no straps, no sensors, no controllers to charge. Just step in frame and play.

  • Full-skeleton tracking at gameplay speed
  • Works with any built-in or USB camera
  • All processing stays on your PC

The setup

Up and moving in
under a minute

  1. 01

    Download & install

    One installer, no account. It bundles the game and the motion engine — nothing else to set up.

  2. 02

    Stand in frame

    Give your webcam a clear view of your body. A quick calibration learns your stance and you're ready.

  3. 03

    Move to play

    March, jump, dodge and squat. Every game reads your real motion and counts every calorie and step.
